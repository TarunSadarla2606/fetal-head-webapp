Automated biometric analysis from 2D ultrasound

About This Tool

This tool performs automated segmentation of the fetal head from 2D obstetric ultrasound images using a deep learning pipeline combining Residual U-Net for robust anatomical boundary delineation and Temporal Attention U-Net for multi-frame consistency.

The system outputs clinically relevant biometric measurements including Head Circumference (HC) in millimetres and Gestational Age (GA) in weeks, derived from standard transventricular and transcerebellar imaging planes in accordance with ISUOG guidelines.

All predictions are accompanied by GradCAM++ visual explainability maps that highlight the ultrasound regions most influential to the model's segmentation decision, supporting clinical transparency and auditability.
